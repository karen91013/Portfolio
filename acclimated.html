Acclimated

Overview

Acclimated is a concept application that seeks to use design and technology in a more personal, human-centered manner. The app gives a predictive reading of what one should wear for the day. It is unique in that it doesn't give the actual outside temperature in the manner of traditional weather applications, but instead gives readings based on what clothes are appropriate for the day and that individual user's preferences. For example, someone who grew up in a hot, dry climate will be more likely acclimated to that type of weather and therefore withstands the cold differently than someone who grew up in in a cold, icy area.
