Civitas Learning Infographic

Overview

Designed an infographic detailing the need for more degrees and education in the United States. This infographic will be digitally distributed to higher education partners and universities and will also be featured on the Civitas Learning website. The target audiences are educators, students, and anyone desiring to learn more about Civitas Learning. This infographic should verbally and visually bring attention to the "urgent need for degrees" in the United States and hint at the solution being presented by Civitas Learning. The brochure should verbally and visually illustrate the product to the target audience in a cohesive manner, as well as adhere to all Civitas Learning guidelines.

*Done in collaboration with Kyle Greenan
